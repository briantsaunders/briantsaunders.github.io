About

Hey! I’m Brian Saunders and this blog primarily centers around technology. A bit of background on me… I started my IT career like many others on the Help Desk. I always had a a fascination around connectivity and how devices communicate with one another both locally and around the world, so my career gravitated towards networking.

Today my focus is still networking but my interests have expanded. These interests include but are not limited to python, javascript, VueJS, linux network stack, and FRRouting. Through this blog I’d like to explore these areas and continue to learn and expand my skillset. Hopefully someone else will find this interesting!

When I’m not tinkering around with technology I like to be with my family, exploring the outdoors, going to old and new restaurants, and really any activity that involves being outdoors and moving around!
